Loans & Savings

Products That Work For You

Explore our range of loan and savings products designed to meet your unique financial needs.

Normal Loan

Borrow up to 2x your savings (3% per month) or 3x your savings (4% per month) for personal needs — education, medical, home improvement, and more. Up to 24 months.

3% – 4% per month (reducing balance)

Loan Within Savings

Borrow up to your full savings balance without disturbing your deposit. Quick processing, lowest rate. Up to 12 months.

1% per month (reducing balance)

Susu Loan

Leverage your susu contributions for affordable credit. Borrow up to 2x (3% per month) or 3x (4% per month) of susu savings. Max GH₵150,000. Up to 12 months.

3% – 4% per month (straight-line)

Business Loan

Grow your business with up to 5x your savings. For inventory, equipment, and business expansion. Up to 24 months.

5% per month(reducing balance)

Salary Workers Loan

Available to salaried workers. Borrow based on your net salary with up to 40% debt service ratio. Up to 18 months.

4% per month(reducing balance)

Controller / DAS Loan

Repayments deducted at source from your salary. Loan amount based on your monthly deduction affordability. Up to 18 months.

4% per month(reducing balance)

Emergency Loan

Quick access to funds for urgent needs. Up to 20% of your total assets, with a ceiling of GH₵500,000. Up to 6 months.

5% per month(reducing balance)

Special Purpose Loan

For weddings, funerals, and other special occasions. Up to 20% of your total assets, ceiling GH₵500,000. Up to 6 months.

5% per month(reducing balance)

Member Savings

To open a Member Savings account, a minimum share purchase of GH₵500 and an entrance fee of GH₵20 is required. Earn competitive interest on your deposits from day one.

Competitive interest

Susu Savings

Daily contributions collected by our trusted field officers. The easy and convenient way to build your savings.

Daily collection

Fixed Deposit

Lock in higher returns with tiered rates based on your deposit amount. Terms: 91, 182, or 365 days. Rates from 5% to 8% p.a.

Up to 8% p.a.

Youth Savings

Secure your child's future with our dedicated youth savings account. Start building their future today.
