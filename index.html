About Us

The Happy Family
You Can Trust

St. Martin De Porres Co-operative Credit Union Ltd. is a dynamic financial institution committed to sustaining the trust, confidence and well-being of our membership through superior products, service excellence, and a highly empowered team of employees.

From our head office at Atonsu, Monaco Junction in Kumasi to branches across the Ashanti Region – we are always close to you, ready to help you grow financially and socially.

Our Mission

Sustaining trust and well-being through superior products, service excellence, and empowered employees for members' development.

Our Vision

To be the premier financial co-operative institution making a positive difference in our members' financial lives.

Our Products

Products That Work For You

Explore our full range of loan and savings products designed to meet your unique financial needs.

Normal Loan

2.4% per month

Borrow up to 2× your savings for personal needs – education, medical, home improvement, and more.

Max: 24 months
Rate p.a.: 28.8%

Loan Within Savings

1% per month

Borrow up to your full savings balance without disturbing your deposit. Lowest rate, quick processing.

Max: 12 months
Rate p.a.: 12%

Susu Loan

2.4%–3.4% per month

Leverage your susu contributions. Borrow up to 2× or 3× of susu savings. Max GH₵150,000.

Max: 12 months
Basis: Straight-line

Business Loan

4.4% per month

Grow your business with up to 5× your savings. For inventory, equipment, and expansion needs.

Max: 24 months
Rate p.a.: 52.8%

Salary Workers Loan

2.4% per month

For salaried workers. Borrow based on your net salary with up to 40% debt service ratio.

Max: 18 months
Rate p.a.: 28.8%

Emergency Loan

3.4% per month

Quick access to funds for urgent needs. Up to 20% of total assets, ceiling GH₵500,000.

Max: 6 months
Rate p.a.: 40.8%

Controller / DAS Loan

2.4% per month

Repayments deducted at source from your salary. Amount based on monthly deduction affordability.

Max: 18 months
Rate p.a.: 28.8%

Special Purpose Loan

4.4% per month

For weddings, funerals, and other special occasions. Up to 20% of total assets, ceiling GH₵500,000.

Max: 6 months
Rate p.a.: 52.8%

Member Savings

Competitive interest

Minimum share purchase of GH₵500 + entrance fee GH₵20. Earn competitive interest from day one.

Min. shares: GH₵500
Entry: GH₵20

Susu Savings

Daily collection

Daily contributions collected by trusted field officers. The easiest way to build savings consistently.

Frequency: Daily
